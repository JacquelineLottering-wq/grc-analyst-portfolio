# Access Control Policy

## Objective
Ensure that access to organizational systems and information is limited to authorized users based on legitimate business need.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who access organizational systems or data.

## Policy Requirements

### Least Privilege
Users must be granted only the level of access necessary to perform their role responsibilities.

### Authentication
All users must authenticate using approved credentials.  
Multi-factor authentication is required for sensitive systems and remote access.

### Account Management
- Each user must have a unique account
- Shared accounts are not permitted
- Accounts must be disabled when access is no longer required

### Access Reviews
Access rights should be reviewed periodically to confirm continued appropriateness.

### Remote Access
Remote connections must use approved secure access methods.

## Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in access removal or disciplinary action.

## Review
This policy should be reviewed annually or following significant system or organizational changes.

## Author Note
Effective access control is one of the most important safeguards against unauthorized system use and data exposure.
