# Risk Register

The following register captures common information security risks relevant to typical organizational systems and user environments.

| ID | Asset | Threat | Weakness | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level | Mitigation Approach |
|----|------|-------|---------|-----------|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| R1 | Customer Database | Unauthorized access | Weak authentication | Medium | High | High | MFA and stronger password policy |
| R2 | Email System | Phishing | Limited user awareness | High | Medium | High | Awareness training and filtering controls |
| R3 | Web Server | Exploitation | Delayed patching | Medium | High | High | Patch management process |
| R4 | User Accounts | Brute force | No lockout controls | Medium | Medium | Medium | Account lockout and monitoring |

## Notes
Risk levels are estimated based on typical likelihood of occurrence and potential business impact if realized.
